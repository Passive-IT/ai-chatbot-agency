AL-BARAKAH RESTAURANT — KNOWLEDGE BASE
=======================================

ABOUT US
--------
Al-Barakah is a family-owned halal Middle Eastern and Mediterranean restaurant.
Located at 78 Crescent Avenue, Greenfield.
Established in 2015. Serving the community with authentic, home-style cooking.
Phone: (555) 876-5432
Email: info@albarakah.com
Website: www.albarakah.com

OPENING HOURS
-------------
Monday – Thursday: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Friday – Saturday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
Sunday: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM
We are closed on Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha (first day only).

HALAL CERTIFICATION
-------------------
All our meat is 100% halal certified.
We source from certified halal suppliers and display our certification in-restaurant.
We do not serve alcohol. Fresh juices, mocktails, and hot beverages are available.

RESERVATIONS
------------
Reservations accepted for parties of 2 to 25 guests.
Call (555) 876-5432 or book online at www.albarakah.com/reserve.
Walk-ins welcome but we recommend booking on weekends.
For groups of 10+, a set menu is required (from $35 per person).

DIETARY INFORMATION
-------------------
All dishes are halal.
Vegetarian and vegan options are clearly marked on the menu.
Gluten-free options available — ask your server.
Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, sesame, and gluten. Please inform us of any allergies.

DELIVERY & TAKEOUT
------------------

Delivery fee: $3 (free for orders over $40).
