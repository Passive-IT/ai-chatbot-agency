ABOUT US
--------
Bella Cucina is an authentic Italian restaurant located at 142 Oak Street, downtown.
We have been serving the community since 2010.
Phone: (555) 234-5678
Email: hello@bellacucina.com
Website: www.bellacucina.com

OPENING HOURS
-------------
Monday – Thursday: 11:30 AM – 9:30 PM
Friday – Saturday: 11:30 AM – 11:00 PM
Sunday: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM
We are closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

RESERVATIONS
------------
We accept reservations for parties of 2 to 20 guests.
To book a table, call (555) 234-5678 or visit our website.
Walk-ins are welcome but reservations are recommended on weekends.
For parties of 8 or more, a credit card is required to hold the reservation.
We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the booking time.

DIETARY & ALLERGENS
-------------------
We accommodate most dietary requirements with advance notice.
Gluten-free pasta is available as a substitute for any pasta dish (+$3).
Vegan options are marked (V) on the full menu.
Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, shellfish, and gluten — please inform your server of any allergies.
We cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment.

PRIVATE DINING & EVENTS
------------------------
We have a private dining room that seats up to 30 guests.
Ideal for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate dinners, and celebrations.
A set menu is required for private events (from $55 per person).
To enquire, email events@bellacucina.com or call (555) 234-5678.

PARKING
-------
Free street parking is available on Oak Street after 6:00 PM on weekdays and all day on weekends.
The Oak Street Garage is a 2-minute walk away (paid parking).

GIFT CARDS
----------
Gift cards are available in any denomination from $25.
They can be purchased in-restaurant or online at www.bellacucina.com/gift-cards.
Gift cards do not expire.

DRESS CODE
----------
